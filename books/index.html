A princess on the run

Princess Lysandra wakes with unshakeable certainty: if she stays at the palace, she will die. She flees, disguises herself, and ends up as a hawk keeper at the estate of a lord she was never supposed to meet.

Alaris is the scandalous lord of the nightlands. He has a stone she needs to survive. She's stealing from him while falling for him.

A masked hunter who never stops

Someone wants her dead. She doesn't know who. She just knows the masked man won't stop.
