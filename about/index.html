Alison Archer

Alison Archer is a traditionally published author writing under a pen name.

When the world went quiet during the pandemic, she fell headfirst into fantasy and romance as a reader, devouring books until 3 AM, and realized the stories she wanted most were the ones she hadn't written yet. SHADOWARD is the first book born from that shift: a murder mystery romantasy about a murder that hasn't happened yet.

She lives in Seattle with her husband. If she keeps you up till 4 AM on a work night because you couldn't put it down, she's done what she came here to do.
